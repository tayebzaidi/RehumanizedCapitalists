Welcome to EatEasy

Find new, healthy recipes with the touch of a button.

Dramatically reduce food waste by using the ingredients from previous recipes for future meals.

With widely available perishable food, it can be difficult to purchase ingridients for a particular recipe without buying more than you need. Much of those surplus ingredients go to waste before they go towards another home cooked meal. EatEasy aims to minimize that waste.

Easily locate ingredients for your recipes.

EatEasy provides an enjoyable interface for you to choose what types of foods you want to eat, giving you the flexibility you want, with the sustainable practices we all need.
